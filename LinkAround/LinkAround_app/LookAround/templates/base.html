Find Opportunities. Discover Talent.

LinkAround connects job seekers and employers through organized portfolio cards, field-based filtering, and location-aware search.

I am a Job Seeker

Build your profile, upload your portfolio, and get matched into field-specific listings that employers browse.

  • Profile and degree details
  • Checklist aligned to your field
  • Portfolio grouped by specialization

I am an Employer

Select business fields, browse candidate portfolio cards, then save top profiles into your private shortlist.

  • Field-based portfolio discovery
  • Private shortlist for selected profiles
  • Direct download of candidate portfolios

Core Platform Functions

Notification System

Job seekers receive updates when an employer picks their portfolio and moves them into a private folder.

Field-Based Database

Portfolios are grouped by specialization or degree to make screening easier for employers.

Location Filter

Employers can filter candidates by area for onsite, hybrid, or remote opportunities.
