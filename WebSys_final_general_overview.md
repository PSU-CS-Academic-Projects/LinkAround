Proposed Final Project: Online Seekers and Employers Portfolio Site

Brief Description:
This concept is meant to be a portfolio/data compiler for people who want to look for a
job in the near future. This would benefit companies that are hiring and looking for a
workforce as well.

How it Works: For Job Seekers
The system will ask you if you’re a job seeker or an employer. If you are a Seeker, you will
be redirected and and let to update and provide your data for the employers; after that,
the system will let you have a checklists in the field that is related to your degree, and
then it’ll automatically redirect your portfolio in the Folder/database with the same field
you’re in.

How it Works: For Employers
The system will ask you if you’re an employer; if you are one, the system will immediately
ask what fields your business covers. If you checked and confirmed all of it, the employer
will be automatically redirected to the folders/database that have seekers’ names and
portfolios in them. If the employer gets interested in the seeker, they can fully download
the portfolio and transfer it into the employer’s personal/private folder for easier
navigation.

Functions:
Notification: Once the employers pick a portfolio and put it in their private folder, the
seeker with that respective portfolio will be notified that they are picked and will be e-
mailed for further details.

Folder/Database: This is where seekers put their portfolios for the employers to assess
and check; it is grouped based on the seekers’ field of preference or their degree in
college. This will make the process more convenient.
Location Filter: This site allows location filtering as well, so if a local company or business
looks for a seeker in a specific area they can easily locate it. This is perfect for every type
of work modality even on-site or work from home.
